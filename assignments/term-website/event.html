Cycle the Continental Divide

The Cycle the Continental Divide, is an annual event that Mountain Spoke sponsors. This is an amazing event were houndreds of cyclists get together to have an amazing experience. From profesional to amateur cyclists participate in this great experience.
Join us in this opportunity if you want to take your bike experience to another level!

Event Registration

Important Information

  • DATES => April 25th, May 1st and May 25th
  • ROUTS => Four Corners Cycling Trails
  • COST => Under 18: $50.00, Over 18: $65.00
  • BASIC REQUIREMENTS => You have to be over 12 years old, have a mountain bike with the corresponding gear (helmet, lights, and comfortable clothing), and lots of energy!!
